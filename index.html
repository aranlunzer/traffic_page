Page purpose

This page hosts supplementary material for the poster Exploring Simulation Scenarios with Timepoint Thumbnails, presented at the IEEE Vis conference in October 2017.

You can look at the poster abstract, and the poster as presented at the conference. You might also be interested in two video-representation works by Robert Ochshorn, that he says led him to the timepoint-thumbnails idea: Chewing and livezoom, both from 2013.
